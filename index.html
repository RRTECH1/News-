About My Photography

Main ek passionate photographer hoon jo duniya ko apne lens ke zariye dekhne ka shauk rakhta hoon. Mera focus portrait aur landscape photography par hai, jahan main har moment ki khoobsurti ko capture karne ki koshish karta hoon.

Mujhe lagta hai ki har photo ek kahani sunata hai. Main har shot mein emotions aur memories ko sametne ki koshish karta hoon, taaki log unhe dekhkar unka anubhav mehsoos kar saken.
